Science fiction author William Gibson’s “The future is already here – it’s just not evenly distributed” and computer scientist Alan Kay’s “The best way to predict the future is to invent it” perfectly express the way we think. Finding the “future” that exists within people today and prototyping that future is the shortcut to creating it. This is the essence of “creating the future”.

Waving a banner with our mission to “Prototype People’s Creativity”, we are on a journey with people representing many different values to create a better future. Through this journey, we believe we can break through oppression and build a world where both individual and societal happiness coexist.

We’re a very small company, but we work with corporate partners and many friends who share our core beliefs. Depending on the nature of a project, we invite the best people from our network to form a professional team.
Because these team members are selected from diverse backgrounds, because we are all conscious of collaborating, and because we are all open to others’ ideas and values, we are able to give rise to creative and innovative ideas that we couldn’t even have anticipated.
We’re always looking for open-minded partners who want to work with us to create a better future. Do you want to create a better future with us?

VOLOCITEE Inc. was founded on September 9, 2011 with partners from a creative community based in Tokyo.
Combining the French word “Volonte”, meaning strong-willed, with the English word “City”, we named the company “VOLOCITEE” to mean a community where strong-willed people gather.
